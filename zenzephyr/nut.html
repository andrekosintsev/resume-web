Instructions

  1. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium-high heat and add some butter to coat the surface.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and whole milk.
  3. Dip each slice of panettone into the egg mixture, ensuring they are well-coated.
  4. Place the coated slices on the hot skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with your favorite toppings.
