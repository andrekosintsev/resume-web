Panettone French Toast Recipe

Ingredients

  • 6 thick slices panettone
  • 4 eggs
  • ¾ cup (177 ml) whole milk
  • Butter
View Full Recipe

Instructions

Step 1: Heat a skillet or griddle over medium-high heat and add some butter to coat the surface.

Step 2: In a bowl, whisk together eggs and whole milk.

Step 3: Dip each slice of panettone into the egg mixture, ensuring they are well-coated.

Step 4: Place the coated slices on the hot skillet and cook until golden brown on both sides.

Step 5: Serve hot with your favorite toppings.
